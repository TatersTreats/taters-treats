Inspired by Tater

From one very loved corgi to treats worth sharing.

Inspired by Tater, our Pembroke Welsh Corgi, we started with a simple belief: dogs deserve the same care, quality, and delight we look for in the things we love most.

That meant creating treats that felt less like generic pet snacks and more like something thoughtfully made. Small-batch. Handmade. Familiar ingredients. A little more bakery than factory.

WOOFLES™ became the first expression of that idea. Crafted with intention, made to feel special, and designed for the kind of dogs who know when something is worth getting excited about.

Tater’s Treats is still growing, but the standard stays the same: make it with care, keep it honest, and build something worthy of the dogs it’s made for.
